Ben Clark

Wexham School

Teaching & Learning CPD		 Ben has been involved in education for a number of years having trained at West London Institute of teaching. He is a secondary trained practitioner and has taught all key stages in a range of subject areas including PE, Dance, Science and Geography. Ben’s areas of specialism as an SLE include
  • developing leadership capacity of colleagues within schools:

  • lesson observation and learning walks;
  • initial Teacher training and induction
  • coaching and mentoring
  • improving behaviour for learning
