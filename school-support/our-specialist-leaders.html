Narinder Basra

Upton Court Grammar School

Maths (secondary)		 Narinder is a Maths teacher, Head of Year, and Staff Governor at Upton Court Grammar School. His areas of interest and specialism as an SLE include:
  • Supporting Maths teachers to improve their subject knowledge and pedagogy.
  • Improving engagement in Maths for all students, by linking the curriculum to the real world and providing a broad range of extra-curricular activities.
  • Improving the provision in Maths for the ‘most able’ students, and enabling more students to go on to read Maths at Oxbridge and Russell Group universities.
  • Supporting his primary colleagues to give more challenge to the most able mathematicians in Years 5 and 6.
  • The use of data and IT to improve progress.
He is an Edexcel Examiner for A-level Maths, and also leads the Slough Maths Network for the AMSP. The aim of the network is to develop the subject knowledge and pedagogy of all teachers of post-16 Maths. Narinder changed career to teaching in 2014 and often refers back to his real-world experience as a working mathematician when advising students on post-18 pathways.
