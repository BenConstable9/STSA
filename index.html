Slough Teaching School Alliance

The Slough Teaching School Alliance is a multiple Teaching School Alliance working in the Borough of Slough and the surrounding area. We provide a range of programmes and activities to support teachers at every stage of their career from initial teacher training through to leadership development.

The Slough Teaching School Alliance is led by designated Teaching Schools working with a range of strategic partners including Slough Borough Council, local schools, higher education institutions and other national organisations.
