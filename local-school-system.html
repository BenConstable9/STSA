  2. Local School System

There are currently 52 schools in total across Slough with a total of around 32,000 pupils. 35 schools are academies, with 12 Multi Academy Trusts and 7 single academy trusts.


Slough Borough Council maintains a website called The Link which describes aspects of the local school system.

Slough Teaching School Alliance works closely with Slough Borough Council and is represented on the Slough Education Partnership Board and the Slough School Improvement Board.
