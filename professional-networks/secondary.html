There are a number of networks across Slough which link together staff working in secondary schools. Some networks, eg those for safeguarding leads or SENDCos, are open to staff from both primary and secondary phases.

Networks may led by Local Authority officers, consultants, SLEs or by other experienced practitioners in schools.
