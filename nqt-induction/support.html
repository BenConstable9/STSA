NQT Support and Professional Development

STSA offers a programme of CPD and support events throughout the year, which NQTs can attend free of charge. Booking should be made via NQT manager by clicking here.



NQT Teaching and Learning Conference, June 5th 2019

There will be a whole day CPD event for NQTs (and RQTs and trainee teachers) on June 5th. The day will include sessions on AfL, managing workload, behaviour management and creativity, amongst other topics. For more details see the flyer here.



NQT CPD Programme 2018-19

The following sessions are included in the NQT package for 2018-19 and so are free of charge. Sessions are open to NQTs from all phases (Early Years, Primary and Secondary).


Session Title Date and Time Details
Survive and Thrive in your NQT Year Mon 15th October
2-4pm		 An opportunity to share effective practice and discuss concerns and queries on a range of topics including:
  • establishing expectations
  • classroom management
  • behaviour
  • managing workload and stress
What Makes Great Teaching? Tues 20th November
4-5.30pm		 What does research highlight as effective classroom practice?
How can we implement this in our teaching?
Effective Communication Wednesday 13th February
4.00-5.30pm		 What makes effective communication with pupils, parents and colleagues?
How can you manage “difficult conversations”?
Moving On: Life Beyond Induction…… Tuesday 26th March
4.00-5.30pm		 An opportunity to think about what happens after the induction period, including career planning and development.
NQT Celebration July 2019 - date TBC Join us to celebrate the successful completion of the induction period.

At times there will be other training events offered by our partnership schools and other providers, which NQTs may be able to attend. Some of these may have a cost and they will be advertised by the schools delivering the training via STSA.
