NQT Support and Professional Development

STSA offers a programme of CPD and support events throughout the year, which NQTs can attend free of charge. Booking should be made via NQT manager by clicking here.



NQT CPD Programme 2020-21

The NQT package for 2020-21 includes a full programme of CPD sessions for NQTs. Sessions will be a mix of half day and twilights, and there will be a blended approach of online delivery and face-to-face sessions. Dates are provisional and will be confirmed in the Autumn term.


Date and Time Session Title Details
Wed 16th September
4.00-5.30pm		 NQT Welcome
2-5pm		 A welcome event for NQTs and their mentors to give an overview of the induction process, and the role of the Appropriate Body and STSA.
Tues 13th October
4.00-5.30pm		 Induction and the Recovery Curriculum The NQT year has always been challenging and this year perhaps even more so. This session will discuss how you can help your pupils and “close the gaps” in their knowledge, including practical actions you can take to support your pupils’ learning.
Date TBC Inclusive Teaching Inclusive practice and quality first teaching – what is best practice for new teachers in how to include pupils with special educational and other needs into their classrooms.
Developing classroom cultures and responses to social, emotional and mental health needs. How can you manage difficult situations with pupils?
Mon 8th February
2.00-5.00pm		 Survive and Thrive An opportunity to share effective practice and discuss concerns and queries on a range of topics including:
  • Classroom management and behaviour
  • Managing workload and stress
  • What makes great teaching? Ideas from research
Tues 23rd March
4.00-5.30pm		 Effective Communication What makes effective communication with pupils, parents and colleagues?
How can you manage “difficult conversations” with parents?
Mon 24th May
4.00-5.30pm		 Moving On: Life Beyond Induction…… An opportunity to think about what happens after the induction period, including career planning and development.
Fri 11th June
8.30-3.30pm		 NQT Conference A whole day conference with a range of sessions focussing on teaching and learning.
Wed 7th July
4:00-5:30pm		 NQT Celebration Join us to celebrate the successful completion of the induction period.

At times there will be other training events offered by our partnership schools and other providers, which NQTs may be able to attend. Some of these may have a cost and they will be advertised by the schools delivering the training via STSA.
